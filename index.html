Innovating the Trade Landscape

Located in the historic and industrial hub of Brasov, Romania, PERFORMINNOVA S.R.L. has emerged as a dynamic leader in the wholesale trade of chemical and allied products. Our company operates at the intersection of traditional distribution and modern logistical innovation, providing a seamless bridge for global manufacturers into the Eastern European market.

What We Do

Our Operational Expertise

Chemical Merchant Wholesale

We specialize in the bulk distribution of chemical and allied products, catering to industrial manufacturers and specialized laboratories. Our team ensures that every shipment meets stringent European safety and environmental standards. We manage complex storage requirements for non-durable chemical components.

Diversified Consumer Goods

Our wholesale portfolio includes a wide range of household appliances, electronics, and home furnishings. We provide retailers with a centralized source for high-demand consumer goods, backed by reliable lead times. We leverage our Brasov location to distribute efficiently across the Romanian territory.

Industrial Supply Logistics

Beyond standard products, we facilitate the procurement of hardware, plumbing, and heating equipment for large-scale infrastructure projects. Our B2B model is designed to support construction and industrial firms with consistent supply of technical components. We simplify the procurement process for complex mechanical assets.
